<p><b>GitLab</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Forge_(software)" title="Forge (software)">software forge</a> primarily developed by <a href="/wiki/GitLab_Inc." title="GitLab Inc.">GitLab Inc.</a>. It is available as a community edition and a commercial edition.
</p>
<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="History">History</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=GitLab&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>
<p>GitLab was created in 2011 by Ukrainian programmer Dmitriy Zaporozhets as a side project written in <a href="/wiki/Ruby_on_Rails" title="Ruby on Rails">Ruby on Rails</a>.
</p>
<div class="mw-heading mw-heading2"><h2 id="Components">Components</h2><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=GitLab&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Components"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>
<p>GitLab consists of different components, mostly interconnected by <a href="/wiki/Unix_domain_socket" title="Unix domain socket">Unix sockets</a>:<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>4<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup>
</p>
<ul><li>GitLab shell</li>
<li>GitLab workhorse</li>
<li>Nginx</li>
<li>Gitaly</li>
<li>Redis</li>
<li>Sidekiq</li>
<li>Database</li>
<li>Unicorn</li></ul>
